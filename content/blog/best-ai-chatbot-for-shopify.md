(Yes, Loqara is on the list — we'll tell you when something else suits you better.)

## What actually makes a chatbot good for Shopify

Feature counts don't matter much. Three things do:

- **Grounded answers** — the agent only answers from your real content, so it never invents a returns policy. ([Why grounding beats the model.](/blog/ai-chatbot-for-online-store))
- **Real Shopify actions** — it can search your catalog and look up orders, not just match FAQs. That's what deflects the "where's my order?" tickets and nudges sales.
- **Clean handoff + fast install** — a [shared inbox a human can jump into](/blog/ai-chatbot-human-handoff), and a one-line embed so you're live the same day.

## The best AI chatbots for Shopify

**Loqara** — *(us)* AI chat **and** voice in one widget, grounded in your content, connected to Shopify for live products and orders, installed with one line. Conversation-based pricing with a real free tier (100/mo). Best for stores that want capable AI support — and optional voice — without a heavyweight helpdesk.

**Gorgias** — the e-commerce helpdesk veteran. Pulls email, chat, and social into one inbox and can automate returns and order edits. Best for **established support teams**; its AI is billed per resolution, so it shines at higher volume.

**Tidio** — friendly, with a visual flow builder. Great for beginners who want to build flows by hand, though its Lyro AI is a paid add-on (see [Tidio alternatives](/blog/tidio-alternatives-for-ecommerce)).

**Intercom (Fin)** — premium and polished, with a strong AI agent priced per resolution. Best if you're a larger SaaS/support org rather than a lean store.

**Re:amaze** — a multichannel helpdesk with native Shopify order actions in the chat; a solid middle option.

## How to pick between them

Roughly:

- **Small / growing store, want AI + maybe voice, predictable cost** → Loqara.
- **Busy multichannel support team, macros and automations** → Gorgias.
- **Love building flows yourself, light automation** → Tidio.
- **Larger org already living in a premium suite** → Intercom or Zendesk.
